👋 Welcome, Teacher!

Welcome to your professional hub, the Teacher Dashboard for Cuambe English School. Here, you can plan lessons, upload learning materials, manage levels, and instantly connect with your students’ dashboards.

This platform celebrates your creativity, leadership, and passion for teaching. It’s built to simplify your planning process, track student engagement, and keep your lessons accessible anytime, anywhere.

💡 Tip: Click on “Levels” to access your course pages (Level 1–5). All files you upload there — videos, audios, PDFs, images, and documents — are automatically shared with students after saving.

📚 Manage Course Levels

Level 1

Fundamental English for beginners: greetings, introductions, routines, and essential communication.

Level 2

Expanding everyday vocabulary, time expressions, and dialogues for elementary learners.

Level 3

Building fluency through storytelling, question forms, and daily conversations.

Level 4

Developing advanced discussions, opinions, and cultural communication skills.

Level 5

Mastering professional English: debates, essay writing, and oral presentations.
